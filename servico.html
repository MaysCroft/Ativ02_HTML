Geo Element

Geo is the Rock Element in Genshin Impact and is one out of 7 Elements in the game.

Geo's only Elemental Reaction is the Crystallize effect, which can interact with all elements except Anemo, Dendro, and itself. Crystal shards created with Crystallize can provide shields when you pick them up! Geo is the Rock Element in Genshin Impact, with Liyue being the designated Geo Region and Morax, or Rex Lapis, as the Geo Archon.

Crystallize Reaction

Geo's only Elemental Reaction is the Crystallize effect, which can interact with all elements except Anemo, Dendro, and itself. Crystal shards created with Crystallize can provide shields when you pick them up!

Geo Resonance - Enduring Rock

Having two Geo characters in the party will activate the Geo Buff called Enduring Rock, which increases shield strength by 15%. Additionally, characters protected by a shield will have their DMG dealt increased by 15% and dealing DMG to enemies will decrease their Geo RES by 20% for 15s.
